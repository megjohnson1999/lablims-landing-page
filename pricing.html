Pricing Questions

Common questions about LabLIMS pricing and plans

Why is pricing customized?

Every laboratory has unique needs in terms of data structures, customization requirements, user count, and deployment preferences. We work with you to understand your specific requirements and provide pricing that reflects the value you'll receive.

What's included in all plans?

All plans include core LIMS functionality (specimen tracking, inventory management, experiment documentation, audit logging), regular software updates, data backup and recovery, and security features. The differences are in customization depth, user limits, and support levels.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can start with a Starter Lab plan and upgrade to Professional or Enterprise as your needs grow. All your data and configurations will be preserved during the upgrade process.

What deployment options are available?

Starter Lab and Professional plans are cloud-hosted for ease of use and automatic updates. Enterprise plans can choose between cloud hosting or on-premise deployment for maximum control and data sovereignty.

Is there a trial or demo available?

Yes! We offer fully-functional demo environments that showcase LabLIMS capabilities for both clinical and animal research workflows. No signup required - just visit our Demos page to explore.

How long does implementation take?

Implementation timeline depends on customization complexity. Basic deployments can be ready in 2-4 weeks, while highly customized Enterprise implementations may take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation process.
